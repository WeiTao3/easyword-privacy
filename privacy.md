# Privacy Policy for EasyWord

**Last updated: April 19, 2026**

EasyWord ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Your **email address** (if you sign up with email)
- Your **name and email** provided by Google or Apple (if you use social sign-in)

### User-Generated Content
We store content you create in the app:
- Words, meanings, pronunciations, and sources you add
- Word lists and their names and descriptions
- Review calendar entries and review history
- Feedback and suggestions you submit

### Audio Recordings (Premium Users)
If you are a premium subscriber and record pronunciation audio, those audio files are uploaded to and stored on our secure cloud servers.

### Usage Data
We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking data beyond what is necessary to provide the app's core features.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Provide and sync your vocabulary data across devices
- Enable account login and authentication
- Store and play back your audio recordings (premium)
- Respond to feedback you submit
- Maintain and improve the app

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

EasyWord uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Database, authentication, and file storage | https://supabase.com/privacy |
| **Google Sign-In** | Optional social authentication | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| **Apple Sign-In** | Optional social authentication | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy |
| **RevenueCat** | In-app purchase management | https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy |

These services may collect limited technical data (e.g. IP addresses, device identifiers) as described in their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.
- If you delete your account from within the app, all your data — including words, lists, review history, feedback, and audio recordings — is permanently deleted from our servers.
- Local data stored on your device (offline cache) is cleared when you delete the app.

---

## 5. Data Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security enforced at the database level — each user can only access their own data. Audio files are stored in a private storage bucket accessible only to the authenticated owner.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

EasyWord is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (all your content is visible within the app)
- **Delete** your data (use "Delete Account" in Settings → Account)
- **Export** your data (use "Export as Text" in Settings → Data Management)

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
